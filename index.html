About me

Raised in the south of Italy, I've always been interested in food, music and technology. I started with web development when Flash was cool but then slowly turned to mobile. I made my first Android app in 2009, and after some experience as freelance specialising in mobile apps I moved to London in 2013 to join the awesome people at novoda as full time Android dev. Berlin is what I currently call home.

I committed to the path of the Software Craftsman, trying to become a better professional through continuous learning and self-improvement. I consider myself an enthusiastic TDD practitioner and a big refactoring freak, but I appreciate clean code as well as implementing beautifully designed UI/UX.

“I love programming. One day you solve the world hunger in ten minutes. Next you spend the entire day making the button blue.” — Daniel Markham
